Meet Our Autonomous Underwater Vehicle

Designed for exploration, innovation, and discovery beneath the waves.

Specifications

DoF 6 (Roll, Pitch, Yaw, Surge, Sway, Heave)
Weight 37 kgs
Dimensions 1051mm x 442mm x 609mm
Endurance 240 Minutes
Depth Rating 150 feet
Pressure Vessels Separate hulls for Electronics, Batteries, Cameras, IMU and DVL.
Actuation System Assembly of eight thrusters, Markers, Gripper and Torpedos
Power Lithium Polymer Batteries
Feedback Control Visual, Inertial, Pressure, DVL and Acoustic

Design

Streamlined hydrodynamic design optimized for underwater efficiency.

Thrusters

Eight high-performance thrusters for precise maneuverability in all directions.

Debug Board

Advanced onboard diagnostics for real-time monitoring and troubleshooting.

Software

AI-powered navigation and data processing for autonomous operations.
